\section{Introduction}

Machine learning can strengthen empirical research when it is used as part of a disciplined scholarly workflow rather than as a stand-alone technical exercise. A publishable machine-learning manuscript should connect a well-framed research query to defensible data decisions, transparent modeling choices, reproducible analysis, and clear interpretation.

This manuscript develops a best-practices framework for moving from query to publication. The intended audience includes researchers, analysts, graduate students, and practitioners preparing machine-learning work for peer review.
