# Submission Package: Claim-to-Evidence Audit Trails for STEM Manuscripts

## Manuscript title

**Claim-to-Evidence Audit Trails for STEM Manuscripts: A Pre-Review Validation Framework for Authors, Editors, and Publishers**

## Article type

Framework / methods / scholarly publishing innovation article.

## Core contribution

This manuscript introduces a STEM manuscript pre-check framework centered on claim-to-evidence audit trails. The framework helps authors, editors, reviewers, and publishers evaluate whether major scientific claims are traceable to appropriate evidence, method support, validation or justification, limitation language, and reproducibility materials before peer review.

## Intended audience

- STEM journal editors
- peer reviewers
- publishing ethics and research integrity teams
- research software engineers
- reproducibility scholars
- scientific authors preparing technical manuscripts
- publishers evaluating AI-assisted or workflow-assisted editorial screening tools
