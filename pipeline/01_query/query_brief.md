# Stage 01 Query Brief

## Working title

From Query to Publication: Best Practices for Publishable Machine-Learning Manuscripts

## Research query

What practices should researchers follow to produce machine-learning manuscripts that are publishable, reproducible, transparent, and useful from the initial research question through peer-reviewed publication?

## Population or audience

Researchers, analysts, graduate students, peer reviewers, and practitioners preparing machine-learning work for scholarly publication.

## Contribution

This project contributes a practical pipeline scaffold that translates established reporting and reproducibility expectations into a repository-based manuscript workflow.

## Scope

The pipeline is general-purpose, with special attention to prediction modeling, applied machine learning, clinical and public-health AI, social-science machine learning, and policy-relevant empirical research.

## Exclusions

This scaffold does not replace field-specific reporting guidelines, institutional review, data-use agreements, or journal instructions.

## Exit gate

Proceed when the research question, audience, contribution, scope, and exclusions are stated clearly enough to guide literature review and protocol design.
