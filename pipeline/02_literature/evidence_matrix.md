# Stage 02 Evidence Matrix

Use this matrix to connect the manuscript pipeline to established reporting standards and reproducibility expectations.

| Source or standard | Domain | Pipeline relevance | Manuscript implication | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| TRIPOD+AI | Prediction models using AI or machine learning | Reporting of model development, validation, and performance | Methods and reporting sections should disclose data, predictors, model development, validation, performance, and limitations | To verify |
| CONSORT-AI | Clinical trials evaluating AI interventions | Trial reporting and human-AI interaction details | Relevant when the ML system is tested prospectively in a clinical trial | To verify |
| SPIRIT-AI | Clinical trial protocols for AI interventions | Protocol specification before evaluation | Relevant when designing prospective AI evaluation | To verify |
| CLAIM | Medical imaging AI | Checklist for AI imaging studies | Useful model for structured AI reporting even outside imaging | To verify |
| Reproducibility and open-science standards | General research | Code, environment, data access, and computational transparency | Repository should preserve enough detail for review and replication | To verify |

## Extraction fields

For each source, capture:

- Citation
- Reporting items
- Required manuscript disclosures
- Required repository artifacts
- Applicability limits
- Notes for this manuscript

## Exit gate

Proceed when every major pipeline stage is mapped to at least one reporting or reproducibility expectation.
