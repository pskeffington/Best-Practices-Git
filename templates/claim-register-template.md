# Claim Register Template

Use this register to track every major manuscript claim against its evidence source and boundary language.

## Claim ID

Example: `C001`

## Manuscript section

Identify where the claim appears.

## Claim text

Write the exact claim or near-final manuscript sentence.

## Claim level

Choose one:

- Descriptive
- Predictive
- Comparative
- Interpretive
- Generalizable
- Actionable

## Evidence sources

List supporting artifacts.

- `artifacts/...`: description

## Validation support

Describe the validation, audit, baseline comparison, or sensitivity check supporting the claim.

## Boundary note

State what the manuscript may not claim.

## Approved language

Write the safest publication-ready version of the claim.

## Reviewer risk

Explain how a reviewer could challenge this claim.

## Revision action

State what additional evidence or wording change would strengthen the claim.
