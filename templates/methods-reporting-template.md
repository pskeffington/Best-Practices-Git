# Machine-Learning Methods Reporting Template

## Research query

State the research question in one paragraph.

## Data source

Describe the source, access date, population, sampling frame, and restrictions.

## Outcome

Define the target variable and justify its measurement.

## Predictors

Describe the predictor set, feature construction rules, and excluded variables.

## Preprocessing

Document missing data handling, scaling, encoding, transformations, and leakage controls.

## Models

List the baseline model and all candidate machine-learning models.

## Validation

Define train, validation, and test splits or resampling strategy.

## Metrics

Identify primary and secondary metrics and explain why they match the research query.

## Interpretation

Describe interpretation tools, subgroup checks, calibration checks, and error analysis.

## Reproducibility

List software versions, random seeds, repository location, and data availability limits.
